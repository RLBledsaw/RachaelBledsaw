Mad Dog Muscle -- founded by trainer Alicia Covelli -- focuses on YOUR goals. Between classes and personal sessions, we help you create a regime that is not only fun, but is made to last. Sound nuts? Well, now you know why we're called Mad Dog.

Alicia trained as not only a personal trainer, but also as a nutritionist. An early devotee of Cross Fit, Alicia specializes in creative circuts centered around body weight. That means limited use of expensive, complicated, machines making the transition to a healthy lifestyle much much easier.

If you prefer to work one-on-one with your trainer, contact us for private sessions. But if you're looking for a fun, energetic, group experiance -- check out our class options!
