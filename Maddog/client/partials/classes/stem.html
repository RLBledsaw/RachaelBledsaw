Strength Toning Endurance Muscle

  • STEM

    • Strength, Toning, Endurance, Muscle is an all over workout meant for those who are looking to get started on the path to the healthier life style. STEM will get you started in a fun, supportive, environment meant to make the transition easier. The STEM class runs for a month, three times a week, for an hour. Although it is geared towards beginners, any fitness level is welcome -- especially if you're looking for an all over regime that's lighter than our Zombie Apoco-prep you can do on your own.

    • Class is $200 (includes a $50 non-refundable deposit at sign up) and includes a booklet on steps to take in diet and lifestyle to help you get started!
    • Class meets Tuesday and Thursday at 5-6:00pm, Saturday at 9-10am.
    • Class is run bi-monthly, space is limited to 15 students.
